Change weapon stats

Change the stats on a weapon. In this case damageMlp.

Example#

self.ragdollHitInfo.hitInfo.damageMlp = 300;

User Variables#

Componentvariable
RagdollHitInfoObjself.ragdollHitInfo

FAQ#

What stats of a weapon can we change?#

The following stats:

Options
templateName
weightAmongMultiple
velocityMlp
damageMlp
damageThrough
damageCrit
stabMlp
hitMlp
hitRandom
stabDamage
breakDefenceMlp
hitBackMlp
knockoutFactor
dizzyFactor
stiffValue

Any way I can revert back to the hitInfo template?#

Use the ResetTemplate function:

self.ragdollHitInfo.hitInfo:ResetTemplate();
